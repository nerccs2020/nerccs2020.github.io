Coronavirus Disease (COVID-19) Update

NEW UPDATE: Given the heightened risk of COVID-19 and a desire to responsibly enact social distancing, NERCCS 2020 will no longer be an in-person conference. We are working to take the conference online and will forward further information as it becomes available. If you have questions, please email: nerccs2020@gmail.com

The NERCCS 2020 organizers have been closely monitoring the outbreak of the SARS-CoV-2 virus and the associated disease COVID-19. We are concerned about the health of the complex systems community and the general public, and we are committed to finding an appropriate compromise between containment of the virus and our goal to support/promote research on complex systems. We will regularly update this webpage with COVID-19 information as it relates NERCCS and will base all decisions on the latest information provided by the U.S. Centers for Disease Control and Prevention and the World Health Organization. We encourage all persons to consult the above websites for a list of best practices to avoid contraction of the virus. We are also monitoring information provided by the University of Buffalo, SUNY (the location of NERCCS).
